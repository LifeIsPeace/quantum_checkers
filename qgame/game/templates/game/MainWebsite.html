What Is Quantum Checkers?

Quantum Checkers is a platform used to teach students about quantum computing through gamification. Our goal is to teach the basics of quantum computing such as: qubits, quantum circuits, quantum entanglement, and quantum gates and how these concepts are related and work together to form the field of quantum computing. We do this by instantiating a quantum circuit, and give the user an image of the goal circuit that they have to reach through the application of various quantum gates onto the starting circuit, making the game an interesting puzzle solving game!

Quantum Checkers Webapp

Description:

Quantum Checkers Webapp is the web application version of the original Quantum Checkers game that was created in Python (see below). It was created by Noah Klaus, Huixiang Zhang, and Eric Smith with the help of Lianxin Xin and supervised by Dr. Ren of Delaware State University. The game consists of solving various puzzles by applying different quantum logic gates onto a quantum circuit consisting of two qubits who are entagled togeter.
